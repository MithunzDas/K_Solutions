Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2025

1. Introduction

At Kasturi Solutions, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website (www.kasturi-solutions.com), engage with our services, or contact us. As a provider of cybersecurity, IT solutions, and managed services, we prioritize data security and compliance with applicable laws.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other details you provide via contact forms or service inquiries.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site, collected via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Service-Related Data: Information provided during service engagements (e.g., business details, technical data) to deliver our solutions.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Deliver and improve our IT, cybersecurity, and managed services.
  • Analyze website usage and enhance user experience.
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent).
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We implement advanced cybersecurity measures, including encryption, firewalls, and secure servers, to protect your data. Our team adheres to industry best practices to ensure your information remains confidential and secure.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics) who assist us, bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings. Learn more in our Cookie Policy (if applicable).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Request details about how we process your data.

Contact us at support@kasturi-solutions.com to exercise these rights.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date.
